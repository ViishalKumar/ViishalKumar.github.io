About Us

Welcome to DudeWhereIsMyNotes! We are your go-to platform for accessing study materials, notes, books, and videos for B-tech students at RTU (Rajasthan Technical University). Whether you're in your first year or final year, we have a wide range of resources to help you succeed in your academic journey.

Our goal is to make learning easy by providing easy access to resources for various subjects like Engineering Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Programming, Communication Skills, and many others!

If you need help finding something, feel free to reach out to us!
